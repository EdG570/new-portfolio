What I do...

Hi, my name is Ed Glazenski and I am a front-end developer from Northumberland, PA who specializes in developing pragmatic web applications . Yes, coding is where my heart lies, but I also have an eye for design and an understanding of the UX/UI process. I don’t just consider myself a developer but rather an overall problem solver. From the moment I wrote my first line of code I was hooked.

My skills include the AngularJS framework, JS, JQuery, and Sass(Bourbon). For my current work flow I am using Gulp, Bower, and NPM along with Git for version control.

Who I am...

Along with coding I am an avid musician and sports fan. I have been playing the bass guitar for about seventeen years now. I truly feel there is a lot of overlap with the logical and creative thinking involved in music and coding. There are many different ways to approach each problem and I am trying to find better ways to improve each and every day.

I am a huge Chicago WhiteSox, San Antonio Spurs, and Seattle Seahawks fan. Strange, isn't it? Someone from Pennsylvania who isn't a Pittsturgh or Philly fan? When I'm not watching my teams or recording a new bass line I enjoy bike riding, softball, and fishing.
