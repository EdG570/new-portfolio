Yoda Weather is a single page app utilizing the AngularJS framework. Weather can be boring so what better way to spruce up the weather than Yoda? Yoda Weather App relies on the Openweathermap API.

Current and five day forecasts are provided for any city in the world. Yoda also offers his take on the weather which will randomly vary depending the on weather conditions.

ABG Wiz is app which makes artertial blood gas analysis easy. When patients are intubated, abgs are regularly drawn to assess adequate ventilation/oxygenation. Therapists have to regularly perform calculations to determine whether adjustments need to be made to balance acid/base in arterial blood.

I developed this app to simplify the calculations, which in turn saves the therapist time.
